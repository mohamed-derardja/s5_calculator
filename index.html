How is this calculated?

1. Module Averages:

  • Crypto, Op. Research, Compilation:
    (Exam × 0.5) + [((TD + TP) / 2) × 0.5]
  • Software Eng, Theory (with TD):
    (Exam × 0.6) + (TD × 0.4)
  • Python, Web Dev:
    (Exam × 0.6) + (TP × 0.4)
  • Theory (Exam Only):
    Exam × 1.0

2. Final GPA:

The sum of (Module Average × Coefficient) divided by the Total Coefficients (19).
